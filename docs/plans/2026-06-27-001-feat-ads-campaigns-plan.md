# Heat Lagos Ad Campaigns - Plan

## Goal Capsule

- **Objective:** Configure and launch three Google Ads campaigns to drive 2-3 leads per day to Heat Lagos without relying on ClassPass, connecting Google Business Profile (GBP) and setting up conversion tracking.
- **Product Authority:** Sebastian Brosche (hello@heatlagos.com)
- **Open Blockers:** None

---

## Product Contract

### Summary
Launch three targeted local Google Ads campaigns (Expats, Tourists, and Local Portuguese) for the Heat Lagos studio with a combined daily test budget of €10 to €15, optimizing for intro offer signups and vacation passes.

### Problem Frame
Heat Lagos needs to steadily acquire 2-3 new leads per day. The studio easily converts students once they experience a class due to the quality of the instruction and infrared setup. However, the studio refuses to use ClassPass. Google Ads will be used to capture local search intent and direct tourists and locals to Bsport without paying ClassPass margins.

### Key Decisions
* **Bilingual Positioning:** Use Portuguese headings and simple English descriptions for the local campaign, reassuring Portuguese speakers that the classes are easy to follow.
* **Low-Barrier CTAs:** Drive traffic to the Intro Offer (2 classes for €22) and Vacation Week (7 days unlimited for €59) rather than high-commitment memberships.
* **Bidding Guardrails:** Set a strict daily budget of €10 to €15 to prevent Google from overspending, optimizing manually each day for click-through rate (CTR) and conversion ROI.

### Requirements

#### Campaign 1: Expats in Lagos
- R1. Copy must be written in English.
- R2. Focus copy on a healthy lifestyle, morning routines, good studio vibes, and a friendly community.
- R3. Primary CTA must promote the Intro Offer: 2 classes for €22.

#### Campaign 2: Tourists & Visitors
- R4. Target tourists visiting the Lagos/Algarve area from the US, Canada, Australia, and the UK.
- R5. Target searchers looking for Bikram, hot yoga, or Pilates who want to maintain their routine on vacation.
- R6. Highlight that Sculpt classes are highly popular, utilizing gentle infrared heat (32°C instead of the typical 40°C).
- R7. Primary CTA must promote the Vacation Week pass: 7 days unlimited for €59.
- R8. Include the practical tip in copy: "Don't forget to bring a towel."

#### Campaign 3: Local Portuguese
- R9. Target Portuguese women aged 35-50 with above-medium household income in the Lagos and Faro region.
- R10. Use Portuguese headlines focusing on "Yoga Quente" (Hot Yoga) but note that instruction is in simple English.
- R11. Position the language barrier with the exact phrase: "Aulas em inglês simples e fáceis de seguir — perfeitas para todos os níveis."

#### Budgeting & Tracking
- R12. Limit combined daily spend to €10 - €15 across all three campaigns during the initial phase.
- R13. Set up Google Ads conversion tracking on `heatlagos.com` triggered by clicks on Bsport scheduling and checkout actions.
- R14. Refine campaign settings daily to optimize for the lowest cost-per-lead (CPL) and maximize class bookings.

### Scope Boundaries
* **In Scope:** Copywriting, local keyword lists, campaign structure, conversion event tracking configuration, and GBP location asset linking.
* **Out of Scope:** ClassPass integration, creating landing pages other than the main `heatlagos.com` scroll sections, and editing Bsport internal checkout code.

### Sources / Research
Lisbon competitor research on "Yoga Quente" (Hot Yoga) reveals that successful studios (Vidya Studio, Yoga Room, Yoga Live Academy) focus on:
* Sweat and detoxification ("libertação de toxinas através do suor/detox").
* Stress relief and muscle relaxation ("aliviar dores musculares e níveis de stress").
* Dynamic flows coordinated with breath ("Vinyasa Flow/fluxo dinâmico").
* Focus, presence, and disconnecting from the outside world ("desligar do mundo lá fora").
